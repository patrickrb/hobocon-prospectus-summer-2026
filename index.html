// What is Hobocon

A conference that moves.

Hobocon is the world's only hacking conference held aboard a moving train. Born from the Kansas City security community, it brings together information security professionals, hackers, researchers, and enthusiasts for an unforgettable multi-day journey of learning, collaboration, and community.

For Summer 2026, we're chartering a private train car on Amtrak's Lincoln Service from Kansas City to Chicago—an approximately 11-hour ride through the heartland. Once in Chicago, a chartered bus brings everyone to our hotel, where the party continues with our friends from Burbsec.

This isn't your typical conference. There are no fluorescent-lit hotel ballrooms. Just 60 hackers, rolling countryside, and 11 hours of uninterrupted community.

// The Route

The Journey

Summer 2026 takes us from Kansas City to Chicago aboard our own private train car.

Morning

Kansas City, MO

Board the Amtrak Lincoln Service at Union Station. Our private car means we set the vibe from the very start.

All Day

On the Rails

~11 hours of hacking, talks, badge soldering, CTF challenges, and building community through the Missouri and Illinois countryside.

Evening

Chicago, IL

Arrive at Union Station. A chartered shuttle bus takes the crew straight to our hotel with a block of rooms reserved.

Night

Chicago Pub Crawl

Team up with Burbsec for a pub crawl. Each bar features a sponsor—collect stamps at every stop, turn in your completed card for a prize drawing on the ride home.

Next Day

The Ride Home

Board the return train. Prize drawings from the pub crawl, lightning talks, and planning next year's adventure.
