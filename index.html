// What is Hobocon

A conference that moves.

Hobocon is the world's only hacking conference held aboard a moving train. Born from the Kansas City security community, it brings together information security professionals, hackers, researchers, and enthusiasts for an unforgettable multi-day journey of learning, collaboration, and community.

On July 17–18, 2026, we're chartering a private train car on Amtrak's Lincoln Service from Kansas City to Chicago and back, approximately 11 hours each way through the heartland. Once in Chicago, a chartered bus brings everyone to our hotel, where the party continues with our friends from Burbsec.

This isn't your typical conference. There are no fluorescent-lit hotel ballrooms. Just 60 hackers, rolling countryside, and ~22 hours of uninterrupted community.
